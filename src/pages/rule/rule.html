Rule

"The game has 2 player which are player and computure or player and player, playing in round"

"In each turn, each player will roll a dice as many time as you desire which the total score of each turn will be added to your ROUND score"

"However, if the player rolls to get " 1 Score", all of your ROUND score will get zero score."

"After that, it will become to another player turn. During rooling a dice, the player can select 'Hold' button to stop this round, which means that player's' ROUND score gets added to his GLOBAL score."

"If any player reach firstly to the limited score that is setted at first, ,that player will win this game "
